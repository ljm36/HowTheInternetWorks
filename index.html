How the Internet works

This is a project for IS117

In this project I'll be taking you around a journey learning a little bit about how the internet works and how we use it in today's age.

Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn are the creators of the internets in the early 1970sThe internet belongs to no one and at the same time everyone. Everyone can send each other packages in between the network depending on the provider that they have with no restrictions. For example, I can be the owner of my own internet by just having people connect to my Wi-Fi with my password. I have the power to give and deny Wi-Fi to the users whenever I please. As well as me going somewhere else and just getting the access to Wi-Fi taken away from me. We use the internet to just communicate, share, apply for jobs, waste time, entertain ourselves, learn, etc. Is endless the use of internet.

We'll learn about domain names and ICANN, as well as the difference between HTTP and HTTPS protocols. We'll dive into the meaning of DNS and learn about IP addresses packets and routing. What is W3C and HTML CSS? We'll find out in the next pages. Click on the button to learn something new about the internet!
